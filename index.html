ABOUT US — WHO WE ARE

Guillem leads the sound-engineering side of Aynix.x. With a background in classical music, trained as a sound engineer at Microfusa Barcelona and full stack dev, specialising in sound design and audio integration solutions for digital art installations, videogames and live shows.

He has worked on installations and media projects with teams such as Ledscontrol, Antelope Audio, Own Spirit, Ibiza Lights festival, Netflix Spain and Guisla Studio among others, covering sound design and technical direction. Within Aynix.x he focuses on making every project carefully designed, clear and easy to translate from studio to stage.

ABOUT US — WHO WE ARE

Fernando focuses on the project management, bookings and event production within the Aynix.x ecosystem. Active in the scene since 2010, he started as a drummer in a rock band and later moved into synthesizers and studio gear, performing Goa trance music under the alias Mush Effect at clubs and festivals across Europe.

He founded Ohmnium Records, organising parties and open-air events in Southern Europe, actually works on the production team at Own Spirit Festival, collaborating on stage programming and on-site coordination, turning the studio work of Aynix.x into a groovy, well-connected live experience on stage.
